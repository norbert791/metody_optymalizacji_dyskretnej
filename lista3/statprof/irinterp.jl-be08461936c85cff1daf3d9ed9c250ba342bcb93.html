17 """
18 mutable struct TwoPhaseDefUseMap
19
20 This struct is intended as a memory- and GC-pressure-efficient mechanism
21 for incrementally computing def-use maps. The idea is that the def-use map
22 is constructed into two passes over the IR. In the first, we simply count the
23 the number of uses, computing the number of uses for each def as well as the
24 total number of uses. In the second pass, we actually fill in the def-use
25 information.
26
27 The idea is that either of these two phases can be combined with other useful
28 work that needs to scan the instruction stream anyway, while avoiding the
29 significant allocation pressure of e.g. allocating an array for every SSA value
30 or attempting to dynamically move things around as new uses are discovered.
31
32 The def-use map is presented as a vector of vectors. For every def, indexing
33 into the map will return a vector of uses.
34 """
